Privacy at a glance

No personal data collection.

The privacy of our users is important to us. ONFNS is designed to operate without collecting, storing, processing, transmitting, selling, or sharing personal or sensitive user data.

01

Information We Collect

ONFNS does not collect any personal information or sensitive user data.

In particular, the App does not collect or process information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other contact information;
  • Account or profile information;
  • Precise or approximate location data;
  • Photos, videos, audio files, or other user files;
  • Contacts;
  • Messages;
  • Financial or payment information;
  • Health or fitness information;
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers;
  • Browsing history or search history;
  • App activity or usage analytics;
  • Crash logs or diagnostic information for developer analytics;
  • Any other personal or sensitive user data.
02

How We Use Your Information

Because ONFNS does not collect or process personal or sensitive user data, we do not use such data for any purpose, including advertising, analytics, profiling, personalization, marketing, or tracking.

03

Data Sharing

ONFNS does not sell, rent, disclose, transfer, or share personal or sensitive user data with third parties, because the App does not collect such data.

04

Data Storage and Retention

ONFNS does not maintain databases or developer-controlled servers containing personal or sensitive information about App users.

Because no personal or sensitive user data is collected by ONFNS, there is no personal user data retained by us and no personal user data that needs to be deleted from our systems.

05

Third-Party Services and Google Play

The App may be downloaded and distributed through Google Play. Google may independently collect and process information in connection with your use of Google Play, your Google account, your device, or the downloading and installation of applications.

Any information collected independently by Google is governed by Google's own terms and privacy policies and is not collected, controlled, or processed by ONFNS.

ONFNS does not use third-party services for the purpose of collecting user data for advertising, behavioral tracking, or analytics.

06

Advertising and Tracking

ONFNS does not use personal data for advertising purposes.

The App does not sell user data and does not use user data for cross-app or cross-service tracking.

07

Children's Privacy and Age Requirement

ONFNS is intended exclusively for individuals who are 18 years of age or older.

The App is not directed to children or minors under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or minors. Since ONFNS does not collect personal information from any users, it does not intentionally collect information from children.

Individuals under the age of 18 should not use the App.

08

Data Security

Because ONFNS does not collect, store, or process personal or sensitive user data, no such user data is maintained on developer-controlled systems.

We nevertheless take reasonable measures to maintain the security and integrity of the App.

09

User Rights

Because ONFNS does not collect or retain personal information, we generally do not possess personal data that can be accessed, corrected, exported, or deleted upon request.

If you believe that you have a privacy-related concern regarding ONFNS, you may contact us using the email address provided below.

10

International Users

ONFNS does not collect or process personal data regardless of the country or region from which the App is used.

Users should be aware that the Google Play platform and other services provided independently by third parties may process information according to their own privacy policies and applicable laws.

11

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if the functionality of ONFNS changes or if changes are necessary to comply with applicable legal or platform requirements.

If ONFNS begins collecting or processing user data in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly before or when such data practices are introduced, as required by applicable laws and platform policies.

Any updated version of this Privacy Policy will include a revised effective date.

12
